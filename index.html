What Are Balayage Highlights?

Balayage, a French word meaning "to sweep," is a freehand hair-coloring technique where the stylist paints color directly onto the hair. Unlike traditional foil highlights, balayage creates a more natural, sun-kissed look that blends seamlessly with your base color.
